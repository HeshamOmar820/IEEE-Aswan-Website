Upper Egypt In Action

A conference that consists of a gallery for projects from Upper Egypt along with several sessions about market, soft and technical skills. The main aim is to enhance both the quality and the number of the projects that contribute on developing our country, in addition to delivering new engineering technologies to Upper Egypt engineering students encouraging them to be creative and giving them the opportunity to show up their work to achieve their dreams.

Mission

Discover new ideas to solve our surrounding problems

Vision

Provide help and support for our engineers and help them take a big step forward

Goal

Give the students in Upper Egypt the chance to show up their work
